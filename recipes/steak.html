Steak

This wonderful garlic butter steak will wow any guest!


Ingredients

  • One Boneless Ribeye
  • 2 Tbps butter
  • 2-4 cloves of garlic
  • A sprig or 2 of Rosemary

Steps

  1. Heat a Cast Iron skillet over medium-high heat
  2. Add 2 Tbps oil on skillet
  3. Add your Ribeye and let it cook for 3-4 minutes
  4. Flip your steak add your butter, rosemary and garlic
  5. Tilt your skillet and begin basting the steak with butter until your desired wellness
  6. Put steak on a plate or tray and cover with aluminum foil for 5 minutes (IMPORTANT)
  7. Serve!
